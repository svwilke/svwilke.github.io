About Me

Hello, my name is Sven! Even before I left school I became passionate about game development. After learning Java, I discovered Unity. It quickly became my favorite tool to create games with. During my time at university studying cognitive science, I kept game development as a hobby. Because crafting joyful experiences is more fun for me than anything else, I decided I want to become a professional.

Apart from game development, I enjoy playing games of all kinds, be it digital, board, or card game. The competitive and social aspects are what I enjoy most about games. Other than that, I like to play table tennis and darts and would not like to live a life without music.



Unity Projects

These are examples of projects I created in Unity. I have provided a GIF for each of them. Simply click the thumbnail to view the GIF!

Tea Time

A point-and-click/puzzle game for cardboard VR created in a group of 6 students for a course at university.

Memory

A simple game of Memory. Features different levels and a mistake mechanic. PC + Android version.

Catch the Goblin

A desktop game where you need to find and catch a goblin in a forest. Created with 4 others for a course at university.

Beyond the Atmo-Sphere

My first Unity game. You control a ball with newtonian physics and have to complete levels in a set amount of time.

Jumper

A prototype for an android game. You charge up a vertical and horizontal force by pressing the left or right side of the screen. Sadly less fun than I thought it would be.
